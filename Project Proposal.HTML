About: My website, Moorprofit.com, is a site to drive awareness, history, and wealth to black businesses in the goal of economically empowering the black community.

Purpose: The purpose is to drive awareness and potentially traffic to black owned businesses through content creation and traffic generation made through blogging on this site.

Target Audience: Moorprofit's target audience is online black consumers that surf the web. Therefore, the content of this site will be geared towards driving them to black owned businesses online.

Content: I plan to make blog posts, videos, and images of successful black entrepreneurs, businesses, and economic history throughout the site. These pieces of content will educate, excite, and entertain the black consumer that is surfing the web.
